IT Technician

Information

Network technicians work to ensure that computers are running properly, they set up networks and routers, they install software and operating systems. They work to ensure that technology is running at effiecient and fast speeds. Jobs in this field can range from installing networks, working to repair damaged computers, and even designing networks and routers.
