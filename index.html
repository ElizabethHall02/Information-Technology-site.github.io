About

Information Technology is a career field that focuses on computers and other forms of technology to store and process data as well as to create and exchange data. It has a wide range of careers in fields such as administration, security, programmming, and and networking to name a few. IT is a field needed pretty much everywhere in the workforce. There are a variety of careers that one can have in the Informtion Technology field, this website aims to help users understand the different options available. Navigating through the different links in this website, you will find careers as well as links to jobs you can have in each.
