Cyber Security

Information

Cyber security is the process of protecting your technology against cyber attacks. This is done in a variety of ways, such as choosing strong passwords, not opening links that seem suspicious, and backing up any important data that you have. A person working in cybersecurity prevents and responds to threats against computers and other technology and helps to recover data from any attacks that may have occured.
